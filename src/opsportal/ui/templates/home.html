Welcome to OpsPortal

Your engineering operations hub

OpsPortal is the single entry point for four internal tools. Each tile on the dashboard represents a distinct application you can launch and use.

Click a tile to launch

Click any application card to auto-start it. OpsPortal manages the lifecycle — it launches the app, checks health, and embeds it in the portal. Use the top navigation to return here anytime.

Monitor health & configuration

Status indicators on each card show whether an app is running, idle, or needs setup. The Health page gives a full system overview. The Settings page shows portal configuration.
